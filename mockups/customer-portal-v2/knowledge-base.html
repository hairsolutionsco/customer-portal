How to Track Your Order

After your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view tracking information in your Orders page. Typical transit times are 3–5 business days for domestic orders.

  1. Navigate to Orders from the sidebar.
  2. Click the order number to open details.
  3. The Tracking section shows carrier, status, and estimated delivery.
